Muffin Tray Rostis

Description

I had a discussion with a friend about making mini potato rostis using a muffin tin.
Culinary experimentation has met with success...





Ingredients

  • 4 Large Desiree potatos (other seasonal varieties can be substituted)
  • Olive oil spray
  • 25g Butter
  • Salt to taste

Method

  1. Pre-heat oven to 200 degrees celcius
  2. Peal and roughly grate the potatoes (store gratings in water to stop oxidization)
  3. Take potato gratings by the handful and squeeze all the water you can out
  4. Transfer squeezed gratings, hand full by hand full to a dry bowl
  5. Season the potato gratings with salt
  6. Lightly spray 2 muffin trays with olive oil (each with 12 x regular muffin cups)
  7. Break up the butter evenly and place 1 small piece in each muffin cup (~1g per muffin cup)
  8. Place muffin trays in oven for 1 minute to melt butter
  9. Remove muffin trays from oven
  10. Divide the potato gratings evenly among the muffin cups and gently press flat with a tea spoon
  11. Return trays to the oven for 20 minutes
  12. Remove the muffin trays and flip the rostis (don't press down, let the air circulate)
  13. Return to the oven for a further 20 minutes
  14. Remove from oven and serve with topping of choice:
  15. Krandor says smoked salmon, creme faiche and finely chopped dill
  16. Vegor says sour creme and chives
